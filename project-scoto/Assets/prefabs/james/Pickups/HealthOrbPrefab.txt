Health energy orb!

DESCRIPTION:
This prefab is set up to to add special effects, sound, and a target to interact with
The Health orb is packed with particle effects to capture the attention of any hungry player, including:
    - Sound effect support
    - Special effect support
    - a target for the orb to track
    - supplemental follow script

USAGE:
To use, drag the Health Orb into the hierarchy!

To add a sound effect drag your favorite sound clip right from 
the project folder into the sound object field.

To add special effects, grab some more particle effects or create your own
and click and drag into the Special effect field.

You can add a target for the follow script by dragging the desired object from the
hierarchy into the target field.
