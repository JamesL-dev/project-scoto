Prefab: Grenade
Purpose: This prefab, when instantiated, creates a grenade in the world with its own rigid body. This grenade interacts with the unity physics system and after a certain amount of time, it explodes, leaving a ring of fire.
Interactions: Once instantiated, it can interact with the world through the unity physics system. Once explodes, it damages any enemies significantly, and leaves a ring of fire which damages enemies over time.
